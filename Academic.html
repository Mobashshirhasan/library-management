Introduction

The AL-FALAH SCHOOL OF EDUCATION AND TRAINING has very gracefully and successfully completed its eight sessions started in 2006 with B.Ed course. The college awarded with grade ‘A’ by NAAC (An Autonomous body of UGC) in 2011.We ensure that the person who will be associated with this ever growing institution will be definitely successful in their future life.
